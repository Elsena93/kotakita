Kota Kita's mission is to improve participatory planning and budgeting processes by making information available to help citizens understand and take advantage of the opportunities that come with rapid urbanization.We'd love your help to reach many more.

Kota Kita works at the grass-roots community level as well as the city government level and often facilitates engagement between a range of stakeholders. We've worked on a wide range of urban issues such as water, urban poverty, mobility and transportation, climate change resilience, participation and urban policymaking.
